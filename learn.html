The Constitution of Kenya
Question 3 of 10 +10 XP per question
Which year was the current Constitution of Kenya promulgated?
2002
2005
2010
2013
Question Explanation
The Constitution of Kenya was promulgated on 27 August 2010, replacing the 1963 independence constitution.

The 2010 Constitution marked a major milestone in Kenya's democratic journey, introducing:

  • Devolution of power to 47 counties
  • Bill of Rights expansion
  • Strengthened judicial independence
  • Land reforms and environmental protections
